八分之幾的乾燥空間
(The Umbrella Project, 2013)

→ Project page on Facebook

2013年4月19日星期五中午，42位學生各自撐著八分之一、八分之二、八分之三、八分之四的雨傘，在台大校園中，從小福走到活大。

At noon on April 19, 2013, a rainy day, each of 42 students holding an umbrella which contains only either one-eighth, two-eighth, three-eighth, or four-eighth fabric, walked from Xiao-Fu Square to the 1st Student Activity Center (280 meters), in the campus of National Taiwan University.

將雨傘依照傘骨八等分分解，打破傘下既有的熟悉空間感，塑造全新的雨天經驗，讓你對傘下的空間有新的體會和想像，發現人傘互動和人際互動的不同可能性。

The umbrella is destructed into eights according to the structure, which breaks the familiar feeling of the space under the umbrella, and forms a new rainy day experience. There would be new understanding and imagination of the various possibilities of interaction between the umbrella and the person and between people.
